The Cauliflower's Cumin From Inside The House


Description

Be hungry. Be very hungry. You’ll want to savor this all-beef patty topped with pepper jack cheese, lettuce, tomato, and of course, the “3 C’s”—a combo of: cauliflower, cumin, and cilantro.


Ingredients

  • Cauliflower
  • Olive Oil
  • Whole Milk
  • Chopped Cilantro
  • Cumin
  • Fresh Lime Juice
  • Ground Beef
  • Pepper Jack Cheese
  • Green Leaf Lettuce
  • Large Tomato, thickly Sliced

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 400F. Seperate the florets from the stem of the cauliflower.
  2. Spread the cauliflower florets on a beking sheet and drizzle with olive oil. Roast in the overn for about 15 mins.
  3. Transfer the cauliflower to a large bowl and add the milk, cilantro, and cumin. mash until you have a chunky mashed potato like texture. Add the lime juice and mix.
  4. Form the beef into patties, season both sides with salt and pepper. While the burgers cook, drizzle lime juice over them. Melt slice of pepper jack on them.
  5. Build your burger: Bottom bun, lettuce, cheeseburger, tomato, cauliflower mash, top bun.
